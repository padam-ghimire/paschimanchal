ADSS Installation

All-dielectric self-supporting (ADSS) cable is a type of optical fiber cable that is strong enough to support itself between structures without using conductive metal elements.

It is used by utility companies as a communications medium, installed along existing overhead transmission lines and often sharing the same support structures as the electrical conductors. We have been providing ADSS Installation service to companies like Nepal Telecom and Ncell.
